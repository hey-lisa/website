---
title: Requirements
description: What you need to use LiSA
---

 
### Wallet
- Self custody EVM wallet that supports EIP 1193 or EIP 6963.
- You sign every action in your wallet. LiSA never asks for seed phrases or private keys.

### Networks and gas
- Major EVM chains are supported.
- Keep a small amount of native gas on the chain you act on.

### Access with $LISA
- Privileged features require a minimum $LISA balance.
- Effective hold equals free $LISA plus $LISA locked under your address.

### Trading connectors
- Hyperliquid runs require your account API key.
- GMX and vault runs request on-chain permissions only when you start a run.
