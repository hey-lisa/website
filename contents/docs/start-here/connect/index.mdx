---
title: Connect
description: Connect your wallet to LiSA
---

 
### Steps
- Click Connect, pick your wallet.
- Sign the one time auth message in your wallet to start the session. It is off chain and costs zero gas.

### What the signature does
- Proves you control the address for this session.
- Creates a session that ends when you disconnect or clear site data.

### What it does not do
- It is not a transaction.
- It does not grant token approvals or move funds.
