### Model
- Deterministic evaluation on a fixed tick, hourly by default.
- Reads market candles from the internal oracle and data plane.
- Emits actions to the selected adapter and records fills, positions, and PnL.

### Adapters
- Hyperliquid adapter places and monitors orders through the user account API.
- On-chain adapter composes calls for swaps and position changes, including GMX and vault flows.
