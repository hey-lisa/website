### $LISA
- Standard ERC-20 on Ethereum.

### External contracts
- Locker, holds locks in $LISA and applies fees or early unlock penalties according to strategy policy.
- Registry, stores strategy ids and sealed spec hashes with policy fields.
- Splitter, distributes $LISA according to creator splits with optional protocol and burn shares.
