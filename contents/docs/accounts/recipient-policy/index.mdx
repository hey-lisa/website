---
title: Recipient policy
description: Control who you can send to
---

 
### Modes
- Open: send to any address.
- Restricted: send only to linked addresses and recipients on your whitelist.

### Set in chat
- Ask LiSA: set recipient policy to Open.
- Ask LiSA: set recipient policy to Restricted.

### Manage recipients
- Ask LiSA: add recipient 0xABCD… label Alice.
- Ask LiSA: remove recipient 0xABCD….
- ENS names are supported where available.

### Behavior
- In Restricted mode, sends to non whitelisted addresses are blocked until you add the recipient or switch to Open.
