### Supported wallets
- Self custody EVM wallets that expose EIP 1193 or EIP 6963 providers.
- Browser injected and mobile wallets are supported.
- ENS names are shown where available.

### Connect and security
- Connect by signing a one time auth message. It is off chain and costs zero gas.
- You sign every action in your wallet.
- LiSA never asks for seed phrases or private keys.
