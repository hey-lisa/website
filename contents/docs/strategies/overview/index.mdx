### Definition
- A strategy is a plan you write in chat that LiSA compiles to a sealed executable spec.
- Strategies are flexible: investing plans, DCA, rebalancing, grid setups, momentum or mean reversion trades, simple rules or multi step systems.

### Where strategies run
- Hyperliquid through your account API, spot or perps.
- On-chain, including GMX and vaults.
