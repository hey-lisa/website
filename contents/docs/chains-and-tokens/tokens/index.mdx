### Token scope
- Native coins and fungible tokens on supported chains.

### Identification in chat
- Symbols and names are recognized in chat.
- You can paste a contract address to specify a custom or unlisted token.

### Approvals
- The first use of a token may require an approval to a spender.
- Approvals are per token and per chain and can be revoked at any time.
