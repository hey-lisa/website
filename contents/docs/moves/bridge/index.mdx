---
title: Bridge
description: Move assets across chains
---

 
### Purpose
- Move assets from one chain to another
- Use when you need funds available on a different network

### Say
- bridge 0.25 ETH from Ethereum to Avalanche
- bridge 500 USDC to Arbitrum

### LiSA extracts
- Source chain, destination chain, token, and amount
- Any intermediate swap required by the route

### Quote
- Total fees and an arrival estimate are shown before you sign
- Slippage applies only if the route includes a swap

### Sign and verify
- Your wallet opens with the bridge transaction on the source chain
- After signing, you see status in the queue and explorer links for both chains
