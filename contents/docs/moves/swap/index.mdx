---
title: Swap
description: Swap assets on a single chain
---

 
### Purpose
- Exchange one token for another on the same chain
- Use when you want a routed price with a minimum received guard

### Say
- swap 1 ETH to USDC on Arbitrum
- swap half my WETH to DAI on Base

### LiSA extracts
- Token in, token out, chain, and amount
- Optional slippage if you specify it in chat

### Quote
- Route, fees, and minimum received are shown before you sign

### Approvals
- The first use of a token may require an approval to a spender
- Approvals are revocable at any time

### Sign and verify
- Your wallet opens with the swap transaction
- After signing, you see status in the queue and an explorer link
