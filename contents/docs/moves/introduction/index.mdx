### Chat interface
- Say what you want in chat
- LiSA reads intent, tokens, chains, amounts, and recipients from your sentence
- LiSA turns the intent into one or more moves and prepares the exact transactions

### Complex asks
- One request can chain several actions, for example swap then bridge
- You can use % and “all/half” in amounts
- You can split across chains or recipients in a single sentence

### Quotes and signatures
- You get a quote with route, fees, minimum received, and a time estimate
- Nothing executes until you sign in your wallet
- If an approval is needed, you sign it first, then you sign the action
