---
title: Revoke
description: Remove token approvals
---

 
### Purpose
- Remove existing token approvals to reduce risk.
- Use for cleanup after trying apps or when tightening a wallet.

### Say
- revoke all approvals on Arbitrum
- show approvals on Ethereum
- revoke USDC approvals on Base
- revoke everything except Uniswap

### Scan and preview
- LiSA scans supported chains for approvals on your linked addresses.
- LiSA shows a list for review and lets you filter by chain, asset, or spender.
- You can remove items before proceeding.

### Prepare and queue
- LiSA prepares the revoke transactions and places them in the queue
- Items on the same chain may be bundled
- See Transaction queue for signing flow specifics

### Fees
- You pay gas for each revoke.

### Safety
- Revoking does not move funds.
- If an approval is needed later, LiSA requests it at that time.
- LiSA warns if a planned revoke would break an active strategy.

### Sign and verify
- Your wallet opens with the revoke transaction.
- After signing, you see status in the queue and an explorer link.
