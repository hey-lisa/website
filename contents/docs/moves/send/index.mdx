---
title: Send
description: Transfer assets to another address
---

 
### Purpose
- Transfer tokens to another address on a chosen chain.
- Use for payments or moving assets between your own wallets.

### Say
- send 0.5 ETH on Arbitrum to 0xABCD…
- send all my USDC on Base to 0xABCD…

### LiSA extracts
- Token, chain, amount, and recipient address.
- Basic recipient checks.

### Recipient policy
- Open: send to any address.
- Restricted: send only to linked addresses and recipients on your whitelist.
- Link an address by connecting that wallet and signing a one time proof.
- You can switch the policy in Accounts and assets at any time.

### Fees
- Only gas is paid for a send.
- There are no DEX or bridge fees.

### Sign and verify
- Your wallet opens with the transfer.
- After signing, you see status in the queue and an explorer link.

### Safety
- LiSA never holds funds or keys. She only prepares the transfer you sign.
