### Definition
- A strategy is a plan that you write in the chat and that LiSA compiles into a sealed executable spec.
- Strategies are flexible, investment plans, DCA, rebalancing, grids, simple rules or multi-step systems.
- Strategies have access to various tools, like most known technical indicators.
- Each strategy is associated with backtest results.
- Strategies can be private, invitation-only, or fully public.
- Strategy creators can monetize public access to their strategies.
- Results of shared strategies are entirely public.
- The operation of strategies can be private or public.

### Where strategies run
- Hyperliquid via your account's API, spot or perps.
- On-chain, via our vaults.
