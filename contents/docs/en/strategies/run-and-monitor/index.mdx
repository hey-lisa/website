### Start
- Review the spec and choose where it runs.
- Launch the strategy when you're ready.

### Monitor
- See positions, recent fills, fees, PnL, and a simple timeline of the run.
- Funds remain on your exchange account or in our vaults under your control.

### Stop
- You can stop a strategy at any time.
