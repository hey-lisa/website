### Strategy backtesting
- Each strategy, once compiled, is executed on historical data.
- You access the REAL historical results of your strategies
- Simulates on historical data with the same tools used live.
- Displays technical data: PnL, drawdown, Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown duration, win rate, profit factor, etc.
