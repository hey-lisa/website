---
title: Bridge
description: Transfer assets across different blockchains using cross-chain bridge protocols. Multi-chain asset movement.
---

### Objective
- Move assets from one chain to another
- Use when you need funds on a different network

### Examples
- send 0.25 ETH from Ethereum to Avalanche
- bridge 500 USDC to Arbitrum

### What LiSA extracts
- Source chain, destination chain, token, and amount


### Quote
- Total fees, routes used, and estimate are displayed before your signature


### Sign and verify
- Your wallet opens with the bridge transaction on the source chain
- After signing, you see the status in the queue and explorer links for both chains
