---
title: Recipient policy
description: Configure recipient restrictions and transaction policies. Define allowed addresses and transfer rules for security.
---

### Modes
- Open: send to any address.
- Restricted: send only to linked addresses and recipients from your whitelist.

### Set in chat
- Ask LiSA
- Or through options

### Manage recipients
- Ask LiSA: add recipient 0xABCD… label Alice
- Ask LiSA: remove recipient Alice/0xABCD…
- ENS names are supported when available.

### Behavior
- In Restricted mode, sends to non-whitelisted addresses are blocked until you add the recipient or switch to Open mode.
