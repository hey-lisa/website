### Wallet
- To access LiSA, you need to connect to the dApp.
- Compatible wallets: EIP 1193 / EIP 6963.

### Networks and gas
- The main EVM chains are supported.
- Keep a small amount of native gas on the chain where you operate.

### Access with $LISA
- Accessing LiSA requires holding a minimum balance of $LISA.
- Effective hold equals $LISA in your wallet + total $LISA locked in strategies

### Trading connectors
- Hyperliquid: API key.
- On-chain: funds are deposited in a smart contract (vault)
