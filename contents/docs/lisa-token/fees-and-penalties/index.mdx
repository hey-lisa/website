title: Fees and penalties (in $LISA)
description: Fee types, penalty, bounds and source
---

### Fee types
- Entry fee at join: fixed $LISA or % of the lock.
- Exit fee at leave: fixed $LISA or % of the lock.

### Penalty
- Early unlock penalty can apply before the minimum period ends.

### Bounds and source
- All fees and penalties are limited by the remaining lock.
- All fees are drawn from the lock. Profits on exchanges or vaults are never touched.
