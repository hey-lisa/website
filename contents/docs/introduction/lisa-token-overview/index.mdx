title: $LISA at a glance
description: Token purpose, role, and status
---

 
### Role
- Access key for LiSA features and related agents.
- Unit for fees, rewards, and penalties in shared strategies.

### Access and locks
- Hold to access, a minimum $LISA balance is required for privileged features.
- Lock to participate, followers lock $LISA to join a shared strategy run, creators lock $LISA to publish.
- Effective hold equals free $LISA in your wallet plus all $LISA locked under your address.

### Fees in strategies
- Fees are $LISA only.
- Entry fee and exit fee can be fixed $LISA or a % of the lock.
- Early unlock penalty can apply.
- Creators define the fee policy in chat, values are stored in $LISA and pinned in the strategy spec.

### Status
- ERC-20 on Ethereum. Fair launch date to be announced.
