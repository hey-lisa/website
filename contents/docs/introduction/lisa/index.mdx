### Definition
- LiSA is an AI web3 agent. You tell it what you want in chat, it turns your words into on-chain moves and trading plans. Always non custodial.

### Why it exists
- Reduce friction for everyday on-chain actions.
- Let you express strategies in simple language and have them executed precisely.
- Keep you sovereign, with your keys, your signatures, and transparent steps you can verify on-chain.

### What LiSA is and is not
- Is: an AI chat interface and execution layer that turns plain language into quotes, routes, and signed transactions for Swap, Bridge, Send, Refuel, and strategy runs.
- Is not: a custodian or a key manager. Funds stay in your wallets, every approval and transfer is user authorized.
