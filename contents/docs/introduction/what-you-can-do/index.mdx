### Ask in chat
- Say what you want in natural language.
- LiSA turns it into one or more transactions you review and sign.

### Moves
- Swap, exchange one token for another on the same chain. The quote shows route, fees, and minimum received.
- Bridge, move assets between chains. The quote shows total fees and an arrival estimate.
- Send, transfer tokens to another address on a chosen chain. Checksums reduce mistakes.
- Refuel, get native gas on a destination chain so you can act there.

### Strategies
- Trading, DCA, or rebalancing plans run by LiSA’s engine.
- You describe the plan in chat. LiSA parses it into an executable spec.
- Runs on Hyperliquid by API, on GMX on-chain, or through an on-chain vault for investing flows.
- When you apply a strategy, choose the funding model, deposit to a vault or use your Hyperliquid balance.
- Backtesting lets you simulate on historical data before running live.
- You can pause, edit parameters, withdraw, and review positions and fills.

### Examples
- Moves, for example:
  - swap 1 ETH to USDC on Arbitrum
  - bridge 0.25 ETH from Ethereum to Avalanche
  - send 25% of my WBTC on Ethereum to 0xABCD…
- Strategies, for example:
  - buy 30 dollars of BTC daily on Ethereum
  - buy when RSI is less than 30, trim 2% on plus 10% days
  - keep 60 to 40 ETH to USDC, rebalance if drift is greater than 5%
